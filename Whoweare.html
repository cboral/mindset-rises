U Se it

To introduce ourselves

"U SE IT" is an association of psychologists, doctors, scientists and professional job mentors. We want to train unemployed citizens who are unmotivated and disoriented to (re) integrate / reintegrate into the labor market. Ki (artificial intelligence), algorithm and positive psychology are part of the big part of our method.

Session content

This course consists of an online-interview, ten online-sessions and a final presentation (given by the client). The interviewer team consists of a psychologist, doctor, scientist and professional job mentor. In the respective sessions, the client has to watch a video or a picture for each session. These videos and images were specifically determined using the AI-artifical intelligence and algorithm ​​technique after during and after the interview. This is followed by a reflective conversation between the client and the professional team. The duration of the session varies between 60 minutes and 180 minutes. Appointments can be arranged relatively flexibly. However in order to determine added value or profit the sessions must not be less than a week and more than 12 days apart. We work with the program Zoom.

Price:

It is totally free for receiver from the "job center" or "Argentur für Arbeit" but you will need a "activation voucher / education voucher". If you have a medical certificate from your doctor or health insurance / Krankenkasse you have to pay only 20% from the total price. In this case a installment payment is possible but you'll not get any discount. If you pay your own shar directly you will receive a 7% discount.
